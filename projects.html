Our Projects

Creating transformative platforms for youth empowerment and community impact

Shika Rada Concert Tours

The Shika Rada Concert Tours are a cornerstone of our youth engagement strategy. These concerts visit three high schools and three universities in each county every school term, featuring live music performances from local and national artists, motivational talks, mentorship sessions, and creative workshops.

Impact:

  • Reaches thousands of students annually across Kenya
  • Provides creative outlets and mentorship opportunities
  • Fosters a culture of community engagement and service
  • Offers platform for emerging artists to showcase talents

Key Features:

  • Community Visitation Days with service projects
  • Interactive workshops on leadership and creativity
  • Networking opportunities for students and artists

Shika Rada Studio

The Shika Rada Studio is a creative hub where young people can explore their talents in media and production. Equipped with the latest technology for recording music, producing podcasts, and creating video content, it serves as the home of the Shika Rada TV Show featuring youth-led conversations.

Impact:

  • Provides hands-on experience in media production
  • Offers platform for youth-led conversations on social issues
  • Encourages collaboration between creative individuals
  • Helps young people develop practical industry skills

Key Features:

  • Shika Rada TV Show with interviews and talk shows
  • "Shika Rada Mitani" segment highlighting youth initiatives
  • Equipment rental for young creatives' projects
  • Training programs in media production

Shika Rada Awards

The Shika Rada Awards is an annual event that celebrates and recognizes outstanding achievements by young people across Kenya. The awards highlight excellence in areas such as music, entrepreneurship, community service, education, and leadership.

Impact:

  • Elevates and celebrates youth accomplishments
  • Inspires others to strive for greatness
  • Encourages a culture of excellence and ambition
  • Promotes the importance of community service

Key Features:

  • Multiple award categories across various fields
  • Nomination process open to the public
  • Gala event with performances and networking
  • Mentorship opportunities for award winners

Shika Rada Foundation

The Shika Rada Foundation is the charitable arm of the initiative, dedicated to addressing the needs of schools and communities across Kenya. The foundation focuses on three key areas: provision of sanitary towels, school fees support, and infrastructure improvement.

Impact:

  • Supports thousands of girls with sanitary products annually
  • Helps financially disadvantaged students complete education
  • Improves learning environments in schools
  • Promotes gender equality in education

Key Features:

  • Sanitary Towel Program for school girls
  • Scholarship and school fees assistance
  • School infrastructure development projects
  • Community partnerships for sustainable impact
