Our Mission & Vision

Empowering Kenyan youth to become leaders, innovators, and positive change-makers

Our Mission

To empower, mentor, and inspire young people in Kenya through creative expression, education, and values-based guidance rooted in biblical principles. We provide platforms for youth to express creativity, build skills, and engage with their communities.

Our Vision

To build a future where Kenyan youth are empowered to become socially responsible leaders, innovators, and agents of positive change. We envision a platform that nurtures creativity, promotes education, and instills values that help young people thrive.

Our Objectives

  • Provide platforms for creative expression in music, media, and arts
  • Mentor youth in educational institutions with life skills and leadership training
  • Offer community outreach and essential services to schools and disadvantaged areas
  • Partner with like-minded organizations to build a strong support network

Our 2030 Roadmap

Strategic goals for national expansion and impact

2023-2025: Foundation Phase

  • Launch concert tours in 20 counties
  • Initiate the Shika Rada Awards program

2026-2028: Growth Phase

  • Establish strong alumni network of leaders
