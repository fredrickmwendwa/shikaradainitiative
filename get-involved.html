Ways to Get Involved

Choose how you'd like to support our mission

Volunteer With Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and make a direct impact in the lives of young people. We welcome individuals with various skills and talents to contribute to our programs.

Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Mentorship and coaching
  • Event organization and support
  • Media and content creation
  • Community outreach programs
  • Administrative support

Fill out the form to express your interest and we'll get back to you with available opportunities.

Support Our Work

Your financial contribution helps us expand our programs and reach more young people across Kenya. Every donation makes a difference in empowering the next generation.

How Your Donation Helps:

  • KES 500 provides sanitary pads for one school girl for a year
  • KES 5,000 supports one concert tour visit to a school
  • KES 10,000 provides partial school fees for a needy student
  • KES 50,000 supports studio equipment for youth training

You can make a one-time donation or set up recurring monthly contributions.

Partner With Us

We collaborate with organizations, businesses, and institutions that share our vision for youth empowerment. Partnering with Shika Rada Initiative provides opportunities for meaningful corporate social responsibility and brand alignment with positive social impact.

Partnership Opportunities:

  • Corporate Sponsorships
  • Program Partnerships
  • Media Collaborations
  • Community Outreach Projects
  • Employee Engagement Programs

Fill out the form to explore partnership possibilities and we'll contact you to discuss further.
