Who We Are

A Christian-based organization empowering youth across Kenya

Shika Rada Initiative is focused on mentoring, educating, and empowering young people (ages 15-35) to navigate life's challenges with confidence, creativity, and integrity. Through music concerts, media production, TV shows, and community service, we inspire the next generation of leaders and innovators.
